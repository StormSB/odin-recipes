Potatoes

Description

A recipe to make the easy danish potato.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Salt

Step by step guide

  1. First peel the potatoes.
  2. Put the potatoes in a pan.
  3. Put water in so it just covers the potatoes.
  4. Put salt in the water.
  5. Let the potatoes simmer boil in 10-20m after the size of the potato.
  6. Take the potatoes of the heat.
  7. Pour the water in to the sink.
  8. Now you have potatoes in a pan!
